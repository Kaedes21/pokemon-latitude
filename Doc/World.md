*Pokemon Chrysanths

**Summary
The story begins in the region of Shansyel, where peace is still unestablished because of differents factions who fight to rule the land, however one rule is shared, Using pokemon for battle is seen as dangerous and even require mandate on some places. Most of the population are farmers, artisan or commercant, making them reliable to their local governor because the usage of pokemon for battle is rare and feared .

On a roaring valley, a boy and his friend Zukian from the village nearby watch a battle opposing two factions , an army under the egid of the Sagare Dynasty and an army of the "Golden Shansyel" Union from the west. However they are discovers by fleeing soldiers from the Union, who want to capture them, Zukian and the boy, who only have one pokemon , flee but ultimately the boy fall from the valley and dive into the water.
